Rainbow Fruit Salad with
Sweet Coconut Cream

Ingredients

Rainbow Fruit Salad

  • 1 whole pineapple
  • 1 papaya
  • 6 Kiwi
  • 2 cup strawberries
  • 2 cup blueberries

Coconut Cream

  • 1 can coconut cream, chilled
  • 1 lime
  • â…“ cup honey

Directions:

1.Prepare fruit. Cut pineapple by removing the rind and core and chopping the edible flesh into bite-sized chunks. Slice papaya in half, scoop out seeds by spoon, remove the flesh using a melon baller or scooping bite-sized pieces out by spoon. Slice kiwi into disks with or without skin. Slice strawberries, removing the stem, and roughly chopping the strawberries.

2.Prepare coconut cream. In a medium bowl, combine the coconut cream and honey. Juice lime and add to the mixture. Whisk until smooth.

3.In a large bowl, combine pineapple, kiwi, papaya, strawberries, and blueberries and stir. Drizzle fruit salad with the coconut cream, or use it as a dip, and enjoy!
